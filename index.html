About Us

Welcome to Ace Tours & Transfers. As one of the leading private hire companies in Bath, we are committed to providing you with a comfortable, reliable, and stress-free travel experience. We offer competitive rates without compromising on quality, operating 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to meet all your transportation needs.

We provide professional airport transfer services as well as long-distance journeys from Bath and the surrounding areas. We operate in full compliance with Bath and North East Somerset regulations, and all of our drivers are DBS-checked for your peace of mind. Whether it’s a work commute, event, conference, sightseeing trip, or air and seaport transfer, for leisure or business, we are committed to delivering an economical, efficient, and prompt taxi service you can rely on.

Important Information

  • Payment Methods

    We accept cash and all major debit and credit cards. All our drivers have in car card machines and we can provide a paper or electronic receipt to your email or phone.

  • Pick up & Drop off

    Airport Transfers: We will arrive at the airport in the arrivals hall 20 minutes after your flight lands and will allow an additional 60 minutes free waiting time after this for you to collect your luggage and use the airport services. Any additional time will incur a parking charge fee. Any Airport drop off fee is included in your quote.

    Taxi services: We will arrive at your preferred pick up destination 10 minutes before your arranged time to help you with any any luggage. We track traffic and weather updates to ensure you are on time and we can update you on any delays.

  • Our fares and fees

    We offer a reliable and affordable service. Simply contact us using our online form, or call us for a quote for your journey. Please include your flight information (for airport transfers). If you are travelling with children and want to use child seats, please bring them with you and we will store them for you for when you get back.

  • Cancellation Policy

    We understand plans can change! If you need to cancel your booking, please note our policy:

    24 Hours Notice: Cancellations made 24 hours or more before your scheduled booking time will incur no charge.

    12–24 Hours Notice: Cancellations made between 12 and 24 hours before your scheduled booking time will be subject to a 50% charge of the total booking fee.

    Less than 12 Hours Notice / No-Show: Bookings cancelled with less than 12 hours' notice, or in the case of a no-show, will be charged the full booking fee.

    To cancel or modify a booking, please contact us as soon as possible.
